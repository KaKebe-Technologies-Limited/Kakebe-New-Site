About Kakebe technologies

Kakebe is one of the market leaders in offering science and technology services to the public in a time -saving and efficient way in Northern Uganda. We provide enhanced IT services to improve productivity and efficiency in businesses, organizations and companies. Building on our technologies, competencies, value and customer interests for our stakeholders and customers. Creating a better everyday life using science and technology.

Kakebe is the central hub of innovations, technology youth involment in technology around the region with headquarters in Lira City. The story of Kakebe started in March 2020 during the COVID-19 outbreak when a team of four vibrant youths saw an opportunity to sell products online due to restrictions but later on birthed into a company that builds softwares and offer different ICT services in Northern Uganda.
