Kakebe Robotics Program

Kakebe Robotics Program

Over the last two decades, the rapid growth of ICT has become one of the most important topics discussed by the scholars in education. This is due to the capability of ICT in providing a dynamic and proactive teaching and learning environment. Increasingly, it is being applied successfully in instruction, learning, and assessment. ICT is considered a powerful tool for educational change and reform

Our Approach

Our practical approach is to add two special IT Special Class fields that we have studied, been part of the program and we value that it makes a lot of difference in the innovation world. The ICT Special Class helps students to learn four main things in terms of discovering, learning how, understanding how and when, and specializing in the use of ICT tools. Our special classes are Robotics & Embedded Systems and Software Development class. Learning institutions, schools and individuals who are passionate in having the skills reach out here Send email inquiry or send WhatsApp

Our Goals

  • Goal 1 - Inspire scientific curiosity by engaging young people in practical technology and innovation activities.
  • Goal 2 Build creativity and improve resilience in young people through STEM activities that use the engineering design process.
  • Goal 3 Youth resilience goal: Engage youth in STEM activities that lead to self reliance and flexible thinking.
  • Goal 4 Problem solving goal. Train Ugandan youth to develop projects that transform their communities by solving local challenges and creating sustainable livelihoods.
