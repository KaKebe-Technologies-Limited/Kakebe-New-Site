Kakebe Internship Program

Internship

Kakebe internship program is a structured work-based learning experience designed to provide students, recent graduates, or individuals seeking to change careers with hands-on exposure to a technology industry or profession. Our internships can be full-time or part-time, paid or unpaid, and may last anywhere from a few weeks to two months

Internship Goal

The primary goal of an internship is to provide the intern with practical experience in a real-world work setting at our head offices in Lira City. This can include tasks such as conducting research, assisting with projects, shadowing professionals, and gaining exposure to the day-to-day operations of the company. Interns also benefit from professional development opportunities such as attending workshops, networking events, and informational interviews. In some cases, internships may also lead to full-time job offers upon completion.

Benefits of Internship

Internship at Kakebe offers a number of benefits to the internee

  1. Hands-on experience
  2. Career exploration
  3. Networking
  4. Resume building.
  5. Skill development
  6. Salary potential
